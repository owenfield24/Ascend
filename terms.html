§ 03

About Ascend

Ascend Golf Camp is a private summer junior golf camp based in San Diego, California, operated by three college students. Each Camp is a three-day program running Wednesday through Friday during the summer months (late May through mid August). The exact venue for each Camp will be announced ahead of time. The Camp is designed for golfers age 18 and under of all skill levels — from first-timers to experienced junior players.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

In Plain English

Full refund if you cancel up to one week before camp starts. Inside that week, no refund — but we may be able to move you to another session.

7.1 Cancellations by You

Cancellation requests must be made in writing by email to info@ascendgolfcamp.com and are effective on the date we receive them. Refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within a reasonable timeframe.

We expect every Camper to treat instructors, fellow Campers, course staff, and the facility with respect at all times. By enrolling a Camper, you agree on their behalf that they will:
