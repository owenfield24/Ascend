— Chapter III · A Day at Ascend —

From sunrise warmups to
course play and closing challenges.

Here's exactly how each day unfolds — structured enough to make real progress, relaxed enough that kids actually enjoy it.

8:30 AM
Roll In & Gear Up
Pull up to the course, meet your coach, snag a fresh Ascend water and snack. You're officially on the clock.
9:00 AM
Warm Up & Wake Up
Loosen up with a guided stretch, then start sending balls down the range. Feel the swing come back to life.
9:30 AM
Full Swing on Trackman
Dial in driver and irons with small-group coaching. Every swing gets tracked, every number tells a story.
11:00 AM
Short Game Showdown
Chip, pitch, and putt across three stations. Sink one from the fringe and you'll feel like a pro.
12:00 PM
Lunch & Hang
Fresh catered lunch in the shade. Trade stories with new friends, talk swings, and get ready for the back half.
1:00 PM
Out on Course
Friday · 9 Holes
Play real holes with your coach walking alongside. Wednesday & Thursday we play a few, then Friday it's a full 9-hole round to put the week together.
2:30 PM
Daily Challenge
Long-drive contests. Closest-to-the-pin. Putting duels. Bring your A-game — winners take home real prizes.
3:00 PM
Wrap & High Fives
Friday · Awards Ceremony
One-on-one feedback from your coach, a quick plan for tomorrow, high fives all around. On Friday at 3:00 PM, we wrap the week with a closing awards ceremony — recognizing standout moments, big improvements, and the week's challenge winners.

Schedule may shift based on weather & course conditions
